ColdStack Rulebook v0.2

Less Delusion.

Protocol Tenets

  1. Build Clean: No backdoors, no bloat, no overengineering. Code, systems, and artifacts must be audit-friendly and minimal.
  2. Evidence Over Emotion: Decisions are based on value, not sentiment.
  3. Deploy Iron Sincerity: Self-aware. Serious.
  4. Default to Open: Unless doing so endangers ops; all tools, protocols, and learnings go public under GPL or Unlicense.
