Protocol Tenets

  2. Drop Quiet: Deploy without drama. Let signal speak louder than branding.

  6. Default to Open: Unless doing so endangers ops, all tools, protocols, and learnings go public under MIT or Unlicense.
  7. Resist Glamour: Avoid status games, follower counts, and corporate co-option.
  8. ColdStack is Not a Brand: It is a daemon, a protocol, a tool. It does not sell. It does not care.
